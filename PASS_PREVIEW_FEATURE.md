## How to Use

1. **Open your XS Card app**
2. **Go to Cards screen**
3. **Tap "Add to Wallet"**
4. **Choose "Preview Pass"**
5. **See your beautiful wallet pass preview!**
6. **Tap "Generate File" to create the actual file**
