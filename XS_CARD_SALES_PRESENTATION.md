## **PRESENTATION TIPS & NOTES**

### **Visual Design Guidelines:**
- **Color Scheme**: Professional blue and gold
- **Fonts**: Clean, modern sans-serif
- **Images**: High-quality, professional photos
- **Icons**: Consistent style throughout
- **Layout**: Clean, uncluttered design

### **Presentation Flow:**
1. **Hook** (Problem) - 2 minutes
2. **Solution** (XS Card) - 3 minutes
3. **Features** (What it does) - 5 minutes
4. **Benefits** (Why it matters) - 3 minutes
5. **Demo** (How it works) - 5 minutes
6. **Pricing** (Investment) - 2 minutes
7. **Action** (Next steps) - 2 minutes
8. **Q&A** (Address concerns) - 5 minutes

### **Key Messages to Reinforce:**
- "Digital networking is the future"
- "XS Card saves time and money"
- "Professional image with environmental responsibility"
- "Easy to use, powerful features"
- "Start free, upgrade when ready"

### **Objection Handling:**
- **"Too expensive"**: Show ROI and cost savings
- **"Too complicated"**: Demonstrate ease of use
- **"Security concerns"**: Highlight compliance and security
- **"Not ready"**: Emphasize free trial and support
- **"Competition"**: Show unique advantages

### **Follow-up Strategy:**
- Send presentation slides
- Schedule product demo
- Provide trial access
- Share success stories
- Set follow-up meeting
