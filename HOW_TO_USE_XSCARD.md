## Installation & Setup

### Download the App
1. **iOS**: Download from the App Store
2. **Android**: Download from Google Play Store
3. **Alternative**: Install via APK file (Android only)

### Initial Setup
1. Open the XSCard app
2. Grant necessary permissions:
   - Camera access (for scanning QR codes)
   - Photo library access (for profile images)
   - Notifications (optional)

## Account Creation & Sign Up

### Creating a New Account
1. **Open the app** and tap "Sign Up"
2. **Fill in your details**:
   - First Name (required)
   - Last Name (required)
   - Email Address (required)
   - Password (minimum 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, and number)
3. **Verify your email**:
   - Check your email inbox
   - Click the verification link
   - Return to the app
4. **Complete signup** and you'll be redirected to the main app

### Password Requirements
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least 1 uppercase letter
- At least 1 lowercase letter
- At least 1 number

## Sign In & Authentication

### Signing In
1. **Enter your credentials**:
   - Email address
   - Password
2. **Optional**: Check "Keep me logged in" for automatic sign-in
3. **Tap "Sign In"**

### Email Verification
- If your email isn't verified, you'll see a verification prompt
- Check your email and click the verification link
- Return to the app to continue

### Forgot Password
1. Tap "Forgot Password?" on the sign-in screen
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Follow the link to reset your password

## Creating Your Digital Business Card

### Adding Your First Card
1. **Navigate to Cards** tab (bottom navigation)
2. **Tap the "+" button** to add a new card
3. **Fill in your information**:
   - **Personal Details**:
     - First Name
     - Last Name
     - Occupation/Title
     - Company
   - **Contact Information**:
     - Email
     - Phone Number (with country code)
   - **Social Media** (optional):
     - Select platforms (WhatsApp, LinkedIn, Instagram, etc.)
     - Enter your usernames/handles
   - **Visual Elements**:
     - Profile Photo (tap to add)
     - Company Logo (tap to add)
     - Color Scheme (choose from presets or custom)

### Customizing Your Card
1. **Choose a color scheme**:
   - Select from preset colors
   - Or create a custom color
2. **Add images**:
   - Profile photo: Tap camera icon or photo library
   - Company logo: Tap logo icon
3. **Adjust logo size**: Use the zoom slider for company logos
4. **Select social platforms**: Choose which social media to include

### Saving Your Card
1. **Review your information** in the preview
2. **Tap "Save Card"**
3. **Success!** Your card is now created and ready to share

## Scanning Cards to Gain Contacts

### Scanning Someone Else's Card
1. **Open the app** and go to Contacts tab
2. **Tap the camera icon** to open scanner
3. **Point camera** at the QR code
4. **Wait for scan confirmation**
5. **Review contact information**:
   - Name, company, phone, email
   - Social media links
   - How you met (add context)
6. **Tap "Save Contact"**

### Manual Contact Entry
1. **Tap "+" in Contacts**
2. **Enter contact details manually**:
   - Name and surname
   - Phone number
   - Email (optional)
   - Company (optional)
   - How you met
3. **Save contact**

### Contact Limits
- **Free Plan**: Limited number of contacts
- **Premium Plans**: Unlimited contacts
- **Check remaining**: See how many contacts you can still add

## Managing Your Contacts

### Viewing Your Contacts
1. **Contacts tab**: Shows all saved contacts
2. **Search**: Use search bar to find specific contacts
3. **Filter**: Filter by company, date added, etc.

### Contact Actions
1. **View contact details**: Tap on any contact
2. **Edit contact**: Tap edit to modify information
3. **Delete contact**: Swipe left and tap delete
4. **Share contact**: Share contact info with others

### Contact Information
Each contact includes:
- **Personal details**: Name, phone, email
- **Professional info**: Company, title
- **Context**: How and where you met
- **Date added**: When contact was saved
- **Linked status**: Whether they're also XSCard users

### Exporting Contacts
1. **Select contacts** you want to export
2. **Tap "Export"**
3. **Choose format**: CSV, vCard, etc.
4. **Save or share** the exported file

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### App Won't Open
- **Restart the app**
- **Restart your device**
- **Check for app updates**
- **Reinstall the app**

#### Can't Sign In
- **Check email and password**
- **Verify email address**
- **Reset password if needed**
- **Check internet connection**

#### QR Code Not Scanning
- **Ensure good lighting**
- **Hold camera steady**
- **Clean camera lens**
- **Try different angles**
- **Check QR code is not damaged**

#### Contacts Not Saving
- **Check contact limit** (free plan has limits)
- **Verify internet connection**
- **Try refreshing the app**
- **Contact support if persistent**

#### Images Not Uploading
- **Check image size** (should be under 5MB)
- **Verify image format** (JPG, PNG supported)
- **Check internet connection**
- **Try different image**
